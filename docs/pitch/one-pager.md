### What it is

MrGovCon turns the federal contracting pipeline into a **calibrated decision queue**. Federal contracting consultancies — the 3,000 boutique firms that help small businesses bid on $200B in federal small-business set-aside contracts — manage opportunities today in spreadsheets. We replace the spreadsheet with a multi-tenant SaaS that ingests SAM.gov, scores every opportunity through a 9-factor probability engine + 6-factor fit + 5-factor market composite, gates on FAR / DFARS compliance, and outputs a BID_PRIME / BID_SUB / NO_BID decision with an immutable audit trail.

### Why now

- **2024 SAM.gov modernization** finally made programmatic ingest reliable
- **2024 SBA size-standard inflation** expanded the SB-eligible pool by ~12%
- **DoD NDAA FY24/25 SDVOSB expansion** doubled the set-aside dollar floor

Three convergent regulatory tailwinds; first defensibly-launched product window since 2018.
