## 1. Beta Status

You acknowledge that the Mercy Raine GovCon Platform is in a pre-release **Beta** state. Features are incomplete, behavior may change, data loss is possible, and AI outputs may be wrong. You participate at your own risk. You are not entitled to ongoing access, feature parity with later releases, or pricing continuity.

**Continued access is conditioned on completing a short weekly feedback questionnaire** (published every Monday at 13:00 UTC). If you do not complete the current week's questionnaire, your access is paused until you submit it. By accepting this Beta NDA you agree that this condition is reasonable and necessary for the data Mercy Raine needs to evolve the Platform.

"**Confidential Information**" includes, without limitation: the existence, scope, design, screen layouts, workflows, prompts, prompt templates, regulatory ontology, scoring algorithms, scoring weights, score-decomposition logic, FAR-foundational architecture, the requirement-extraction pipeline, the compliance-matrix grid, the proposal editor's three-pane design, the FAR-grounded prompt scaffolding, the audit-event design, any roadmap or feature plans shown to you, any benchmarks, any performance data, any pricing not yet publicly announced, source code, object code, screenshots, screen recordings, demo recordings, exported reports' templating, internal documentation, and any other non-public information you observe through Beta access.

## 3. Obligations

You will:

1. **Not disclose** Confidential Information to any third party, including consultants, contractors, journalists, investors, competitors, or AI services. The only exception is your own employees who (a) need to know to operate the Platform on your behalf and (b) are bound by written confidentiality terms at least as protective as this Beta NDA. You remain liable for any breach by such employees.
2. **Not publish, post, blog, tweet, or screenshot** any element of the Platform — UI screen captures, prompt outputs, scoring results, regulatory ontology rows, or any other artifact — to any social, professional, or public forum.
3. **Not copy, reproduce, or extract** any Confidential Information except as strictly necessary for your authorized internal use of the Platform. You may not download, scrape, or otherwise systematically extract regulatory data, prompts, scoring logic, or AI outputs for use outside the Platform.
4. **Not recreate, replicate, clone, fork, mirror, or build any product, service, prompt library, regulatory dataset, or scoring system** that derives from your access. This restriction is the central consideration for granting Beta access; you acknowledge you would not have been admitted without it.
5. **Not feed Confidential Information into any third-party AI or ML system**, including but not limited to commercial LLM providers, fine-tuning platforms, or vector databases, except for your authorized use within the Platform itself.
6. **Not use Confidential Information to advise, fund, hire, or otherwise assist** any person developing a product or service that competes with or substitutes for the Platform.
7. **Protect Confidential Information** with at least the degree of care you use for your own most sensitive trade secrets, and never less than reasonable care.
8. **Promptly notify** Mercy Raine in writing of any actual or suspected unauthorized disclosure or use, and reasonably cooperate to mitigate.

## 4. Excluded Information

These obligations do not apply to information you can demonstrate by contemporaneous written records was: (a) in your possession before disclosure by Mercy Raine without obligation of confidentiality; (b) independently developed by personnel without access to Confidential Information; (c) generally available to the public through no fault of yours; or (d) lawfully obtained from a third party without obligation of confidentiality. The exclusions do not apply to source code, prompts, regulatory ontology data, or scoring algorithms, all of which are presumed proprietary.
