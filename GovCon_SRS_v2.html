1. Product Overview & Mission

The GovCon Advisory Intelligence Platform is a multi-tenant SaaS application purpose-built for government contracting consulting firms. It automates opportunity discovery, applies AI-driven analysis to solicitation documents, and generates actionable bid intelligence — replacing manual research workflows that currently consume dozens of analyst hours per pursuit.

The platform ingests live federal opportunities from SAM.gov, scores them using a probabilistic 8-factor model, and surfaces only the highest-value matches per client. Consultants manage the full pursuit lifecycle — from opportunity identification through bid decision, proposal planning, submission tracking, and compliance monitoring — in a single workspace.

Launch Goal: Enable a consulting firm to onboard a client, ingest relevant federal opportunities, receive AI-scored bid recommendations, and generate a compliance matrix and bid strategy document — all within 15 minutes of first login, with no manual data entry.

3. Core Feature Modules

Opportunity Intelligence

Live SAM.gov ingestion, AI scoring, enrichment from USASpending, win probability display with disclaimer, deadline urgency classification.

AI Document Analysis

Upload RFP/SOW/amendment documents. AI extracts scope keywords, complexity score, alignment score, and incumbent signals. Triggers re-scoring.

Compliance Matrix

AI generates a structured requirement-by-requirement compliance checklist from solicitation text. Each row is trackable with status, notes, and proposal section assignment.

Bid Guidance

AI produces a plain-language win strategy including discriminators, risks, teaming recommendations, pricing guidance, and past performance hooks.

Bid Decision Engine

8-factor logistic probability model with Bayesian calibration produces BID_PRIME / BID_SUB / NO_BID recommendations with full factor breakdown per client-opportunity pair.

Client Portal

Branded portal view for end-clients. Shows their active opportunities, bid decisions, submission history, and uploaded documents relevant to their engagements.

Analytics & Forecasting

Pipeline funnel, win distribution, penalty trends, submission velocity, Monte Carlo revenue forecast, client portfolio diversification (HHI index), and market intelligence.

Rewards Program

Points-based incentive system. Clients earn rewards for on-time submissions, compliance milestones, and referrals. Redeemable through the admin panel.

4. AI Intelligence Layer

The platform operates an AI abstraction layer that routes all AI calls through a central provider router. Each firm independently selects their preferred provider and stores their own API key. The system supports four providers with automatic failover.

4.3 Usage Tracking

Every AI call is logged with provider, model, task type, input/output token counts, estimated cost (USD), cache hit flag, and latency. Admins view a 30-day usage summary broken down by task and by provider in Settings. Cache hits are logged at $0 cost.

4.4 LocalAI Integration

LocalAI runs as a containerized service alongside the platform. It exposes an OpenAI-compatible REST API with no external key required. If LocalAI fails, the platform automatically falls back to Claude. The backend transparently rewrites localhost URLs to the internal Docker hostname so consultants can enter the external URL in Settings regardless of deployment environment.

5. Opportunity Management

5.1 Ingestion & Enrichment

  • Trigger SAM.gov ingest with optional NAICS code, agency, and set-aside filters (up to 100 results per run). Concurrent duplicate jobs are blocked.
  • Post-ingest, opportunities are automatically queued for probability scoring.
  • Enrichment job fetches historical award data from USASpending: incumbent, award history, competitor count, agency small-business and SDVOSB rates.

5.2 Probability Scoring — 8 Factors

NAICS Alignment Set-Aside Match Incumbent Probability Competition Density Agency Relationship Document Intel Score Deadline Urgency Penalty Drag

Scores are Bayesian-calibrated using the firm's historical win/loss record. All displayed probabilities carry an "est." disclaimer badge.

5.3 Opportunity Detail View

  • Full solicitation metadata, POC, place of performance, set-aside eligibility
  • Document upload (PDF, DOCX, TXT, ZIP) with automatic AI analysis on upload
  • Score breakdown panel with per-factor contribution visualization
  • Compliance Matrix and Bid Guidance tabs generated on demand
  • Amendment tracking with plain-language AI interpretation per amendment
  • Client matching: run bid decision engine against any active client
  • Export Synopsis: professional HTML export with agency cleanup, deadline urgency badge, probability gauge, score breakdown bars, AI scope keyword tags, and competitive intelligence grid

6. Client & Pipeline Management

6.1 Client Profiles

  • Business certifications: SDVOSB, WOSB, HUBZone, Small Business, 8(a)
  • NAICS codes (multi-value) for opportunity matching
  • Performance stats: submitted, won, on-time rate, total penalties
  • Document requirements checklist with compliance status tracking
  • Active/inactive status with per-client penalty schedule override

6.2 Bid Decisions

Generated per client-opportunity pair. Includes recommendation (BID_PRIME / BID_SUB / NO_BID), win probability, plain-language explanation, and full factor breakdown. Consultants can override and add notes. Decisions feed the analytics pipeline funnel.

6.3 Submissions & Penalties

  • Submission records track status, on-time flag, and contract value
  • Penalties auto-calculated on late or non-compliant submissions per firm penalty schedule
  • Penalty drag applied in real time to probability scoring via exponential decay: exp(-totalPenalties / 200,000)

6.4 Client Portal

Clients access a read-only branded portal showing their opportunity pipeline, bid decisions, and submission history. Portal tokens are scoped exclusively to the authenticated client and cannot access any other firm or client data.

7. Analytics & Forecasting

Pipeline Funnel

Ingested to Scored to Decided to Submitted to Won. Real contract values at each stage with conversion rates.

Win Probability Distribution

Histogram of scored opportunities by probability band. Identifies where the pipeline is concentrated.

Revenue Forecast

Monte Carlo simulation (1,000 runs) over active pipeline. P10 / P50 / P90 confidence bands with HHI diversification index.

Market Intelligence

NAICS sector trend detection via linear regression. Agency profiles with small-business award rates and competitive landscape.

Submission Velocity

EMA-smoothed trend of submissions over time. Late submission probability via Beta distribution model.

Risk Radar

Flags deadline risk, compliance blocks, high late-submission probability, and unpaid penalties requiring immediate attention.

Smart Match Engine

Automatically surfaces top client-opportunity pairings ranked by combined probability score with NAICS pre-filter.

Penalty Trend

Time-series of financial penalties applied across the portfolio. Identifies compliance risk patterns per client over time.

8. Compliance & Audit

  • Compliance Logs: Every status change on opportunities, submissions, and documents generates an immutable audit log entry with entity type, from/to status, triggering user, and timestamp.
  • Compliance Matrix: AI-generated requirement checklist per opportunity. Each row tracks requirement text, FAR reference, mandatory flag, status (NOT_STARTED / IN_PROGRESS / COMPLETE / WAIVED / NON_COMPLIANT), assigned proposal section, and notes.
  • Document Requirements: Per-client checklists for certifications, past performance packages, and financial documents with due dates and compliance status.
  • Compliance Report Export: Date-range filtered export of all audit events for a firm.

9. Settings & Administration

9.1 API Key Management

All API keys stored per-firm in the database. Masked in the UI with reveal toggle. Saving a key immediately refreshes the stored-key confirmation row. Supported keys: SAM.gov, Claude (Anthropic), OpenAI, Insight Engine. LocalAI requires only a base URL and model name — no key.

9.2 Firm Configuration

  • Penalty schedule: flat late fee (USD) and/or percentage of contract value
  • SAM.gov sync preferences: default NAICS filter and result limit
  • Active AI provider selection applied firm-wide
  • User roster management with role display
  • Template library: upload, approve/reject, and assign document templates to clients
  • Demo seed: one-click realistic data population for demos and training

9.3 AI Usage Dashboard

Collapsible panel in Settings showing last 30 days: total calls, total tokens, estimated cost (USD), breakdown by task and by provider. Cache hits shown at $0 cost.

10. Non-Functional Requirements & Launch Readiness

10.1 Security & Multi-Tenancy

  • Every database query scoped by consultingFirmId extracted from JWT — no cross-tenant data leakage is architecturally possible
  • Client portal tokens scoped exclusively to their clientCompanyId
  • Role-based access control enforced at the route middleware level
  • API keys never returned in list endpoints — only masked previews exposed

10.2 Reliability & Resilience

  • All AI provider failures return graceful fallbacks — no application crashes
  • LocalAI failures fall back to Claude automatically if a key is available
  • Background jobs run in isolated workers with error capture and status tracking
  • Uploaded files persist in a named Docker volume independent of container lifecycle
  • Redis caching (7-day TTL) for compliance matrix and bid guidance reduces redundant AI cost
